We are very happy to welcome Ashley Cozine to our production. She is a New York University Tisch School of the Arts alum. Here, she studied at the Lee Strasberg Theatre & Film Institute and Stonestreet Studios.

Ashley is currently appearing in SAG AFTRA New Media web series "Look Up in the Sky," as well as feature film "A Perfect Life" and short films "By the Numbers," "Dessert and Suicide," "On the Mountain," and "Till Death Do Us Part." Since 2011, her films have been seen in over 30 public screenings including the Valley Film Festival, Beverly Hills Shorts Festival, Traverse City Shorts Festival, Seattle True Independent Film Festival, Tacoma Film Festival, Gig Harbor Film Festival, Local Sightings Film Festival, Columbia Gorge International Film Festival, and Park City Film Music Festival and numerous independent screenings in Seattle and Los Angeles. Prior to this, she curated and hosted the NYU Tisch West Alumni Council's Cinema Series at AFI for three seasons while working on a number of independent and major motion pictures.

Ashley will be working on social media for Since '45X and will also be our on-set production manager. We are very happy to have her on-board.
